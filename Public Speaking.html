PUBLIC SPEAKING

A way of connecting to people directly!

Public speaking has been traditionally known as speaking live in front of an audience. Today, with advancing times, public speaking can be also done virtually. Both in-person and virtual public speaking have almost the same challenges besides technical barriers, nonetheless the impact of speaking to an audience remains unmatched as you serve a message through to more people at one and that is the sole purpose of public speaking.

Public speaking has 3 main purposes being:

  • To educate👩‍🏫
  • To persuade or motivate🏆
  • To entertain🤹‍♀️

The common barriers with public speaking are lack of confidence, nervousness or fear of being rejected. All of these barriers are internal and can be overcomed with the help of a mentor and practice over time using a mirror alone and advanced to a small audience then a bigger one.
